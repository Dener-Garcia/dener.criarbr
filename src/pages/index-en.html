A Bit of History

I started my journey in the industry developing projects for HMIs (Human-Machine Interfaces), from there I focused on improving and sophisticating the design of my screens by applying UX/UI concepts and never stopped.

I am currently a post-graduate in UX/UI Design, focused on combining business, technology, and people through design, creating intuitive, accessible interfaces focused on solving problems.

As a UX Designer, I deliver all stages of a digital product that range from the discovery phase with stakeholders, handoff with the development team, to usability testing. I have participated in several projects from different niches, ranging from institutional websites, mobile applications, web platforms to the creation of design systems.
