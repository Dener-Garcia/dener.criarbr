Being a Reference in Energy Solutions

Project Objective

Create the digital presence of Company Geradores through a fully responsive website, generating value for its users.

Kick off

UX Process

The project was defined to be a one-page website, I focused heavily on accessibility, color contrast, and conveying information clearly and objectively, doing strong UX writing work.

Design

Ideation and Code

A simple layout but with a lot of information to be conveyed, I structured most in the form of cards and topics, with the main highlight on contact buttons and the types of services offered by the company.

Handoff

Ready for Code

Component-based development always facilitates the quick and precise delivery of design.

One of the project requirements was the use of blue and orange colors, which had to be adapted for screen use. As I focused on a clean design, I had no problems during development.

Challenges

Learnings

The main goal was to bring more leads to the company and demonstrate who the company is.

SEO had to be very well thought out in the construction of the page, using appropriate semantics in the code and, above all, maintaining a design consistent with the brand's proposal.
