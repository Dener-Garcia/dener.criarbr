Promote an artificial intelligence

Project Objective

Create a modern landing page for an artificial intelligence company highlighting sections such as company presentation, products offered, how the product works, customer testimonials, and a contact form.

Kick off

UX Process

Based on the mood board and other design references, I sought to build a simple page with few sections but that presents the new product of a company working with interactive AI for chats.

Design

Ideation and Prototyping

Eye-catching at the right point! In this project, I was able to bring colors that refer to technology and innovation such as blue, pink, and purple.

I used a common 8-point grid and brought a hierarchy of information like images, titles, and buttons to clearly separate the sections of each part of the page.

Handoff

Ready for Code

As designers, we have the responsibility to think about performance as well; creating an attractive interface that is possible to "code." In the end, a good user experience is linked to several other factors like loading response and interactions.

Challenges

Learnings

Although the theme of artificial intelligence is on the rise, seeking references was fundamental to the project's success. We had a short deadline for the project, but since we had the product well defined, I could concentrate on the visual and technical appeal of the page.
